Who we are

This website (jhchandanagar.growingmonk.com) is owned and operated by Growing Monk on behalf of Jawed Habib Hair & Beauty — Chandanagar, located at 4-92, First Floor, Above Happi Mobiles, Beside GHMC Office, Main Road Chandanagar, Hyderabad — 500050, Telangana, India.

Growing Monk is a digital marketing agency that builds and manages this website. References to "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to Growing Monk acting on behalf of the Chandanagar branch.

What information we collect

We collect information only when you actively provide it to us, or automatically through standard web analytics tools.

Appointment booking form

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Preferred service, date, and time
  • Any optional notes you choose to include

Analytics (automatically collected)

  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Referral source (how you found us)

We do not collect payment card details, ID documents, or any sensitive personal information through this website.

How we use your information

  • To process and confirm your appointment booking via WhatsApp
  • To log bookings in an internal spreadsheet for salon scheduling
  • To understand website performance and improve the user experience
  • To measure the effectiveness of our marketing

We do not use your personal information for unsolicited marketing, sell it to any third party, or share it beyond what is described in this policy.

Third-party services

This website uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Google Tag Manager & Google Analytics — we use these to understand how visitors use the site. Data is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy.
  • WhatsApp (Meta) — when you submit the booking form, your details are sent to the salon's WhatsApp number. Meta's Privacy Policy governs that communication.
  • Google Sheets (via Google Apps Script) — booking details are also logged to a private Google Sheet accessible only by the salon and Growing Monk.
  • Google Fonts — the Inter typeface is loaded from Google's servers; Google may log this request.
  • Google Maps — the map embed on our homepage is served by Google.

Cookies

This site uses cookies primarily through Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager. These are small text files stored in your browser that help us count visits and understand traffic sources.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Doing so will not prevent you from using the site, but analytics data from your visit will not be collected.

Data retention

Appointment booking data is retained in our scheduling spreadsheet for up to 12 months, after which it is deleted. Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months as per our Analytics configuration.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you have submitted through this site. To make a request, contact us using the details below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

External links

This site contains links to external platforms (Instagram, Facebook, JustDial, JH Salons national website). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies directly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
